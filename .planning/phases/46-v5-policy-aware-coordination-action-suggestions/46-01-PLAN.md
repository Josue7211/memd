# Plan 46-01: `v5` Policy-Aware Coordination Action Suggestions

## Goal

Help richer operator surfaces move from bounded coordination pressure to the
most appropriate bounded next action without removing operator control.

## Steps

1. Derive compact bounded action suggestions from current coordination state.
2. Keep the first slice aligned with current inbox, recovery, policy, and history categories.
3. Reuse existing coordination policy and ownership signals instead of inventing a second recommendation model.
4. Preserve explicit operator choice: suggest, do not auto-execute.

## Verification

- richer operator surfaces receive bounded next-action suggestions from current pressure
- suggestions stay aligned with existing coordination policy and ownership signals
- operator control remains explicit

## Result

Coordination surfaces become more assistive without becoming opaque automation.
