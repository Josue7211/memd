# Plan 37-01: `v5` Coordination Policy and Ownership Guards

## Goal

Add explicit coordination policy and ownership guardrails on top of the current
peer coworking substrate.

## Steps

1. Define lightweight coordination modes such as exclusive write, shared review, and help-only.
2. Surface policy mismatches before sessions step on the same scope or task.
3. Keep the first policy slice compatible with existing claims, tasks, and inbox flows.
4. Preserve inspectable operator control instead of hidden coordination heuristics.

## Verification

- sessions can distinguish exclusive and collaborative work lanes clearly
- policy mismatches surface before overlapping work causes conflict
- task and claim flows remain compatible with existing coworking commands

## Result

Peer coordination grows from recoverable ownership into policy-aware coworking.
