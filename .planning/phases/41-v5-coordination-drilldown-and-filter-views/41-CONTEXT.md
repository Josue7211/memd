# Context 41: `v5` Coordination Drilldown and Filter Views

## Why This Exists

Phase 40 added a cleaner coordination dashboard, but active coworking pressure
will quickly outgrow one compact overview. Operators and peer agents need a
bounded way to focus on the right slice of coordination state without rereading
the entire dashboard every time.

## Inputs

- phase 35 compact inbox and task presence
- phase 36 stale-session recovery
- phase 37 coordination policy guards
- phase 38 branch and scope recommendations
- phase 39 coordination receipts
- phase 40 dashboard and history views

## Desired Outcome

Add narrow, filterable coordination drilldowns so operators can inspect only the
slice they care about: inbox pressure, help/review requests, recovery issues,
policy conflicts, recommendations, or recent receipts.
