# Phase 47.35: `v6` Large-Context Workflow Compression — Plan

## Goal

Make genuinely large jobs manageable by splitting them into reusable layers
instead of forcing giant context on every intermediate turn.

## Plan

1. Keep a layered large-context workflow.
2. Preserve provenance and cross-chunk traceability.
3. Keep intermediate turns compact.
4. Reserve broad passes for reconciliation and harmonization.

## Verification

- `cargo fmt --all`
- `cargo test -p memd-client`
- large-context guidance exists in roadmap and docs

## Result

Short and medium sessions stay competitive with huge sessions for most long-
form work.
