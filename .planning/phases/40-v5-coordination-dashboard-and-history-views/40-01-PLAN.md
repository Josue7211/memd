# Plan 40-01: `v5` Coordination Dashboard and History Views

## Goal

Add cleaner operator-facing history and dashboard views on top of the current
coordination substrate.

## Steps

1. Turn the current coordination summary into a more dashboard-like view with clearer sections.
2. Add bounded history inspection for recent receipts and coordination pressure.
3. Keep the first slice compatible with existing CLI and MCP surfaces.
4. Preserve compactness and inspectability over verbosity.

## Verification

- operators can inspect current pressure and recent history faster than raw JSON browsing
- the dashboard/history view remains bounded and useful under active work pressure
- receipt and coordination data stay aligned across the different surfaces

## Result

Peer coordination becomes easier to inspect live and retrospectively.
