# Plan 39-01: `v5` Coordination Audit Trail and Receipts

## Goal

Add compact coordination receipts so coworking actions stay inspectable over
time.

## Steps

1. Record compact coordination receipts for assignment, recovery, help, review, and transfer actions.
2. Expose a bounded audit view through CLI and MCP surfaces.
3. Keep the first slice compact and structured instead of turning into a raw transcript log.
4. Preserve alignment with existing task, claim, and inbox flows.

## Verification

- coordination actions produce compact, inspectable receipts
- operators can review recent coworking transitions without reconstructing them from raw state
- the audit surface remains bounded and useful under active work pressure

## Result

Peer coordination gains a durable audit trail without losing compactness.
