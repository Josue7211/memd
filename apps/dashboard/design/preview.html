Design DNA

Superhuman

Purple as singular accent. Tight display compression. Binary radius system. Confidence through emptiness.

SpaceX

Zero shadows. Uppercase taxonomy labels. Ghost interactives. Spectral text. Mission-critical restraint.

Warp

Warm dark canvas. Semi-transparent borders. Regular weight dominance. Editorial spacing. Monochromatic calm.
